Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Volume? ▼

    Volume is a website designed to help individuals improve their speaking skills.

  • How can I sign up for Volume? ▼

    You can sign up for Volume by visiting our registration page and following the instructions.

  • Is Volume accessible? ▼

    Yes, Volume offers both dark and light themes, as well as three different font sizes for accessibility.

  • Are there any fees for using Volume? ▼

    No, Volume is completely free to use for all users.

  • Can I customize my profile on Volume? ▼

    Yes, Volume allows users to customize their profiles with personal information, profile pictures, and preferences.

  • How can I reset my password on Volume? ▼

    You can reset your password on Volume by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and following the instructions provided.
